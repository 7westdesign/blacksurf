About Us

I started Pike Surf School so that I could pass my love and knowledge of surfing on to this generation and the next, to anyone and everyone who is interested in learning. My hope and dream is to inspire others, so they can connect with their sense of wonder and zest for life through surfing. Over the years, I've given lessons to everyone from children, teens, and adults - and the one thing I've always found to be true is that having a good firsttime surfing experience is key. But there is also something that keeps us all coming back for more, the drive to be a surfer. I want to share that experience with you.

Meet The Instructor

James Benjamin Pike, born on July 11th ,1985, at the Children’s Hospital in beautiful San Francisco, California. When I came out of the womb, the doctor held me up by my feet, looked me up and down, then handed me over to my Mother and said ” He’s going to be a skateboarder.” My Mother, in horror, covered my little ears and said ” Don’t say that!” . The doctor reply, “Sorry, I’ve been doing this for a 40 years, I can just tell, Skater. ” My parents were living in Fremont, California at the time, my Mother was working at the Port of Oakland, as a Management Consultant, and it was easy for her to break away from work to cross over into San Francisco for doctor visits and eventually my birth. My Dad was also a Management Consultant in Silicon Valley. My Mom left her employment at the Port of Oakland and stayed home with me until the age of six-years old. In 1992, she decided to return to her original profession: University Associate Professor.
