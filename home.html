The Mission:

As the Founder of Pike Surf School, I set out to create a safe place where anyone could learn how to surf, in the most enjoybale way possible. To me, learning how to surf is more than just learning how to ride a wave or how to paddle (we teach that too :) but learn about what it truly means to surf. Having grown up in Santa Barbara, I've given lessons to everyone from children, teens, adults and Grandparents, and the one thing I've always found to be true is that having a good firsttime surfing experience is key. But also, there is also something that keeps us all coming back for more, the drive to be a surfer.
