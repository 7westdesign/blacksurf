Whether you're looking to stand up for your very first time or looking to fine-tune your skills or become more well-rounded, Pike Surf School is your best choice for fun, safe and affordable surf lessons in Santa Barbara!

About Lessons

James Pike has been teaching surfing lessons for over 12 years and is CPR and First Aid Certified. Pike’s Surf Academy offers a friendly and fun setting to learn how to surf, so that anyone who lives in town or is passing thru, has a wonderful surfing experience. This is a sport ANYONE and EVERYONE ages ( 4-77+ ) who has the call to try it. This is your opportunity of a life time to learn how to ride a surfboard with style, in a comfortable setting and in the company of an expert. Give the gift of surfing to yourself and your loved ones, and enjoy your first-time experience together! Set up a surfing lesson today and learn how to surf with Pike!

Lesson Information:

Each private surfing lesson are 120 minutes (2 hours) long and includes: wetsuits, surfboards with surfing leash and 1 0n 1, personalized learning environment with surfing instructor James Pike. Surf lessons can be set up by phone (805-698-7630) or email (contact@pikesurfschool.com). Please include your full name and the height’s and weight’s for the people in the surf lesson, as well as a good time and date that works for you. Pike’s Surf School accepts for payment of Cash and Checks (Please make checks payable to “Pike’s Surf School”).

Locations:

We currently offer surf lessons at the following two locations: Santa Barbara and Santa Cruz, California. To book a surf lesson at any of these locations, please call (805-698-7630) or email pike.schoolofstyle@yahoo.com
