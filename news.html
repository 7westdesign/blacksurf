About Pike Surf School

As the Founder of Pike Surf School, I set out to create a safe place where anyone could learn about surfing, not just how to learn how to ride a wave or paddle (we teach that too :) but learn about what it truly means to be a surfer, from the ground up. I've given lessons to everyone from children, teens, and adults, and the one thing I've always found to be true is that having a good firsttime surfing experience is key.
