Lesson Info:

James Pike has been surfing and teaching surfing lessons for over 12 years and is CPR and First Aid Certified. Pike’s Surf ‘n Skate goal is to be an asset to the world, so that anyone who lives in town or is pasting thru, has a wonderful surfing experience. This is a sport for ages ( 4-77+ ). This is your opportunity of a life time to learn how to ride a surfboard with style, in a comfortable setting with an expert. Give the gift of surfing to yourself and your whole family, so everyone is involved and having fun! Set up a surfing lesson today and learn how to surf with Pike!
